Why are animals in danger of extinction?
Image result for animals in danger of
extinction reason Species become endangered
for two main reasons: loss of habitat and
loss of genetic variation. A loss of habitat can
happen naturally. Dinosaurs, for instance,
lost their habitat about 65 million years ago.



While its numbers are slowly increasing,

The Health Factor

Even reducing meat intake has a protective effect.
Research shows that people who eat red meat are at an
increased risk of death from heart disease, stroke or
diabetes. Processed meats also increase the risk of
death from these diseases. And what you don't eat can
also harm your health

Is it healthy to stop eating meat?
Myth. According to the National Institutes
of Health, a number of studies over the years
linked red meat with an increased risk of cardio
vascular disease, some cancers, and diabetes. But
other research shows that a low level of consumption
– no more than 2-3 servings per week – is not deter
